Cultures of Conversation: Communication Tools

Communication is how we find ourselves in connection with the other. It is the bedrock of building relationships and culture. There are many ways in which we [can] communicate: Verbal (Emotional and Logical), Kinesthetic, Intuitive, Visual, and Sensual.

Tools and practices can help us develop more empathy and compassion in conversations, while effectively expressing ourselves.
