Cultures of Co-Creation: Tools for Collaboration

I am interested in co-creating cultures which are equitable and non-hierarchal. Cultures where everyone is honored in their roles and the gifts they naturally offer. Co-creation is a concept where we recognize that cultures are formed collaboratively, where everyone plays a crucial & sacred role. The inherent creativity of each individual finds a place in the collective process with harmony, and brings about an emergence of rich cultures.

We find ourselves in the world where most organizations are led in hierarchy and this hierarchy creates social status, exclusivity and fractured cultures of less-than & more-than. We lose our sovereignty as human contributions are reduced to limited metrics, and cultures become competitive rather than collaborative and co-creative. This page compiles some tools & practices which can help us reconfigure beliefs & communications for co-creative cultures of richness & vibrancy.
