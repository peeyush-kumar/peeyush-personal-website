Far beyond the unceasing impressions
There is a space of dreams that sings
The subtle song of which holds the many into one
Like roots of the tree holding the soil
And soil in turn holding the tree

Every morning when I wake up
And every night when I go to sleep
I hear this eternal song
This moment I invite you to listen to this song with me
This is your song
A song of the World - a song of creation, and a song of belonging
A song of eventually returning home with celebration

Song that carries the seeds of the world our hearts desire

Song that shows the joy of responsible action

Song that brings courage and gentleness to stand with sovereignty & in service
To co-create collectively a story of Humanity
